Cranberry Salad

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 bag fresh cranberries
  • 1 medium (17 oz maybe?) can crushed pineapple
  • sugar
  • red seedless grapes
  • chopped walnuts (optional)
  • whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Coarsely chop the cranberries. (A food processor works well for this.)
  2. Thoroughly drain the pineapple.
  3. Quarter the grapes. (1/2 a pound, or more if you like grapes).
  4. Mix fruit with 1/4 to 1/2 cup sugar.
  5. Add chopped walnuts if desired. (1/2 a cup to 1 cup, or even more if you like walnuts)
  6. Let sit overnight.
  7. 10-60 minutes before serving:
  8. Whip the cream. (1 small carton, or a little more. If desired, whip 1 or 2 teaspoons of powdered sugar in with the cream.)
  9. Fold the whipped cream into the fruit.
