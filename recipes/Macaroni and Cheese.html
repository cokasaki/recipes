Macaroni and Cheese

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) box (gluten-free) elbow macaroni (or other pasta)
  • 1 pound extra sharp cheddar cheese (grated)
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Heat water to boiling in a big pot.
  2. Add pasta to boiling water. Stir to get the clumps broken up. Cook for about 7 minutes, stirring occasionally. (This time is for elbow mac -- other pasta shapes may take longer.)
  3. While pasta is cooking:
    • Grate cheese (if necessary).
    • Beat eggs in a bowl. Add about 2/3rds of the evaporated milk and mix.
  4. Take out one piece of pasta. Blow on it to cool, and tilt&shake it to get out any water trapped inside. Taste it to make sure pasta is done.
  5. When pasta is done, drain in a colander, then put pasta back in pot.
  6. Add grated cheese to pasta and stir to mix.
  7. Over low heat, add the egg-milk mixture to the pasta and cheese. Stir quickly so that eggs don't scramble.
  8. Continue to cook, stirring frequently, for a few minutes until cheese is melted and sauce is creamy. During this time, if the sauce seems too thick, stir in some of the remaining milk.
