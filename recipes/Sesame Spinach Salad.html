Sesame Spinach Salad

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 package fresh spinach (10 oz)
  • 3 spoons soy sauce
  • 2 spoons sesame oil
  • 1 spoon roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 spoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Blanch the spinach for 30 seconds in boiling water. The spinach should turn bright green.
  2. Immediately run under cold water. I do this in about 3-4 batches for the bag of spinach. I use my straining spoon to get the spinach out of the water and into the strainer.
  3. Squeeze out the excess water -- grab a handful and squeeze it into a ball.
  4. Cut each ball into smaller pieces, about every ½”.
  5. Mix the dressing in a bowl. Note that I use one of the large silverware spoons.
  6. Loosen the pieces of spinach and add to the dressing.
  7. Mix.
  8. Chill.
