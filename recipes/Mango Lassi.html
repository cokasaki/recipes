Mango Lassi

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¼ cup water
  • ¾ tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ cup fresh or canned mango pulp(see note)
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom seeds(plus a little extra for garnish)
  • 3 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth. Add sugar to taste. Garnish with a pinch of ground cardamom seeds. A few extra ice cubes may be added when serving.
