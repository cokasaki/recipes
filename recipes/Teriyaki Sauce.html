Ingredients

  • 1⁄4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 5 tablespoons packed brown sugar
  • 1 -2 tablespoon honey

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients, thicken with
  2. 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  3. 1⁄4 cup cold water
