Chickpea and Avocado Salad

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 medium can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1-2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 avocado, peeled, seeded, and cubed

Instructions

  1. Mix chickpeas, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.
  2. Let marinate for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. A few minutes before serving, add the avocado cubes, and stir to coat the avocado with the dressing.
