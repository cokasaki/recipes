Granny’s Spiced Nuts

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1C. Sugar
  • 1 t. Cinnamon
  • Dash salt
  • 1 ¼ t. Vanilla
  • 7 T. water
  • 2 C. Pecans (or walnuts)

Instructions

  1. Mix everything except nuts. Boil to soft-ball stage without stirring.
  2. Stir in nuts.
  3. Cook until they start to turn to sugar.
  4. Turn out on buttered foil and separate.
