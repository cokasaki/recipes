Coconut Lime Rice

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1/4 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • salt, to taste
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until the water runs clear; drain.
  2. Heat coconut oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook and stir rice and coconut flakes for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the lime juice, then add the coconut milk, vegetable broth, salt and lime zest.
  5. Bring to a low boil, then cover and reduce heat to low. Cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and keep covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with fork and season with pepper.
