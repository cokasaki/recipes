Roasted Beet Goat Cheese Salad

Back to Top

Ingredients

  • Some beets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt/pepper
  • Arugula (can substitue spinach or some other green)
  • Dried cranberries
  • Some fruit (apples, or crisp pears, work best)
  • Goat cheese

Instructions

  1. Roast the beets
  2. Put it all together!
