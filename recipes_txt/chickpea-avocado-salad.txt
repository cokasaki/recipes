Chickpea and Avocado Salad

A chickpea and avocado salad

Salads
Vegetarian
Gluten-Free
Nut-Free
Sesame-Free
Peanut-Free

1 medium can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1-2 teaspoons olive oil
1-2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 avocado, peeled, seeded, and cubed

Mix chickpeas, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.
Let marinate for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
A few minutes before serving, add the avocado cubes, and stir to coat the avocado with the dressing.
