Granny’s Spiced Nuts

Spiced nuts!

Dessert
Family Recipes
Gluten-Free
Peanut-Free
Sesame-Free

1C. Sugar
1 t. Cinnamon
Dash salt
1 ¼ t. Vanilla
7 T. water
2 C. Pecans (or walnuts)

Mix everything except nuts. Boil to soft-ball stage without stirring. 
Stir in nuts.
Cook until they start to turn to sugar.
Turn out on buttered foil and separate.
